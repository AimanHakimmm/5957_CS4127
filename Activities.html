Things to Do

Discover the best activities to do in Japan

Tea Making

Tea making in Japan, especially the preparation of matcha, is an art form rooted in tradition. It emphasizes mindfulness, precision, and aesthetics.

Relax in Onsen

Experience Japan's traditional hot springs, known as onsen, offering relaxation and scenic views while enjoying Japan's natural beauty.

Enjoy Nature

Japan offers diverse natural landscapes, from Hokkaido’s winter skiing and hot springs to Okinawa’s tropical beaches and coral reefs. Seasonal highlights include Mount Fuji, cherry blossoms in spring, and vibrant autumn foliage.

Shop in Osaka

Explore Dotonbori and enjoy vibrant street food.

Experience Mount Fuji

Hike or admire the iconic peak from nearby spots.
